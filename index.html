About Us

Dzivhani Unarine

Founder of BrightDev

Campus Shop was created to empower students with opportunities to develop entrepreneurial skills while making money. Our platform enables students to buy and sell products within their campus community, fostering a marketplace where learning meets earning.

Our mission is simple: to make opportunities available equally for all students. We believe every student should have access to affordable goods and the chance to develop business skills that will serve them throughout their careers.
